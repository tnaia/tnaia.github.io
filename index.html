PhD — University of Birmingham, 2018.




Hello!

I am a María de Maeztu fellow at the Centre de Reserca Matemática,
working with Perarnau, Guillem and the GAPCOMB-UPC group.

Previously, I was a postdoc at LaBRI-U. Bordeaux and IME-USP,
working with Marthe Bonamy and Yoshiharu Kohayakawa.

I received my PhD from the University of Birmingham, in 2018,
where my supervisors were Richard Mycroft and Deryk Osthus.

I currently supervise Hugo Vicente (MSc, since 2024, co-supervised by Guilherme Mota).

I am interested in Combinatorics, Probability and Algorithms.
